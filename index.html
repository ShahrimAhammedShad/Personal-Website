About

Law, literature, teaching, and technology in one working life.

Shad studies Islamic law and general law at Islamic University, using legal education as a way to understand justice, society, governance, and the philosophical foundations of human civilization.

My name is Shahrim Ahmmed Shad. Law is not merely an academic discipline for me; it is a field through which I seek to understand justice, society, governance, and the philosophical foundations of human civilization.

I am deeply interested in legal history, constitutional theory, political thought, jurisprudence, and the evolution of legal systems, especially across South Asia.

I aspire to write books and novels that combine philosophy, psychology, social analysis, and human experience through themes of love, rebellion, identity, morality, and the complexities of the human mind.
